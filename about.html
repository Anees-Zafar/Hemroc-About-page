Our Processing Units

What Make Us Special

2014

The Start of Hemroc Traders

Hemroc Trading (Pvt.) Ltd. was founded in 2014 by a group of safety-focused professionals committed to delivering unparalleled trading services across various industries. With a vision to provide top-quality products and services, Hemroc quickly established itself as a trusted name in the trading sector. Our journey began with our first office located at University Road, Near Agriculture Office, Mardan, KPK, serving as the central hub for our operations and customer interactions.

2016

Honey Processing Excellence

Our journey into processing began with the establishment of our honey processing unit on Main GT Road, Tarnab, Peshawar, KPK. This facility was designed to harness the rich beekeeping traditions of the region, allowing us to produce premium quality honey. The unit focuses on meticulous filtration and moisture reduction processes, ensuring that the honey we deliver is pure, rich in nutrients, and meets international standards. This unit has become a cornerstone in our commitment to natural and healthy products.

2017

Mangoes and Vegetables Storage and Packing

To diversify further, we launched a facility near Faisal Mover, Wihari, Multan, Punjab, dedicated to the storage and packing of mangoes and vegetables. This unit leverages modern storage technologies to maintain the freshness and quality of produce. By carefully sorting, cleaning, and packing the produce, we ensure that it reaches consumers in prime condition. The facility plays a crucial role in our supply chain, particularly during peak harvest seasons, allowing us to deliver farm-fresh products to markets across the globe.

2019

Advanced Rice Milling Operations

Expanding our capabilities, we set up a state-of-the-art rice mill along Sahiwal Road, Near Lari Adda, Arifwala, Punjab. This mill is pivotal in processing various rice varieties to perfection, catering to the diverse preferences of our clients. By integrating advanced machinery and precise quality control measures, the mill ensures that every grain of rice processed reflects our dedication to excellence. From cleaning to polishing, each step is designed to preserve the nutritional value and taste of the rice.
